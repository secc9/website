Ashley Sagar

Experimental artist and musician exploring at the fringes of the mainstream.

I am an artist and musician experimenting in digital and analog technologies and processes.
My research is focused on exploring composition using the act of process and limited means.
I perform regularly as an improvising musician, drawing on techniques from jazz, afro, indian, latin and eastern musics as well as exploring diy hacking/building and self made electronic music devices.
Since before beginning my formal academic education I have had a passing intrest in computer code and programming, but whilst never following any formal educational route I instead approached programming from the angle of an artist, using the smallest amount of technique to aim to produce work. This website is an attempt for me to break that cycle and formally follow through on a more "traditional" approach to coding and programmming.
