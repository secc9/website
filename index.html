Ashley Sagar

Experimental artist and musician exploring at the fringes of the mainstream.

I am an artist and musician experimenting in digital and analog technologies and processes.

My research is focused on exploring composition using the act of process, recursion and limited means.

I perform regularly as an improvising musician, drawing on techniques from jazz, afro, indian, latin and eastern musics as well as exploring diy hacking/building and self made electronic music devices.
